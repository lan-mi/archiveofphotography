Additional programs

During Women Photographers in Vojvodina in the XIX and the first half of the XX century exhibition, we organized different additional programs, including lectures and guided tours. Our guest lecturer, Melanija Lojpur, together with the professionals from our team, analyzed the content of the exhibition within the frameworks of philosophy, gender, history of photography and general history. We collaborated with local school organizing the visits to the lectures and tours. The gallery also hosted a band Gradovi utočišta, from Novi Sad (Ivan Čkonjević, the guitar, Tijana Stanković, the violin and Milica Svirac, the cello).
