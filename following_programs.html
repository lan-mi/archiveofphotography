Following programs

During Woman photographers in Vojvodina in the XIX and the first half of the XX century exhibition, we organized following programs. There were several lectures and guided tours. Our guest lecturer Melanija Lojpur, together with professionals from our own team, analyzed the content of the exhibition from the philosophy, gender, history of photography and general history perspectives. We collaborated with local school and had an organized visit to the lectures and tours. There was also a concert in the great ambiance of the gallery space. The XXX band from Novi Sad (...and Milica Svirac) gave a great performance.
