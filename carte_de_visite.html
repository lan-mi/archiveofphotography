Carte de visite

Most of the events that promote care for photographic cultural heritage take place in cultural institutions. We wanted to bring this heritage to so we decided to bring 100 years old photographs to the main city square. We dressed up accordingly and then walked around the city center inviting people to come to see the photographs. We let them examine the photos themselves and told them the stories behind the pictures. People were quite intrigued by the photos and tried to find a connection between the scenes from the photographs and the present time. Some of the visitors had their own stories to share. We were happy to hear them.
