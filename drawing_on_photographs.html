Drawing on photographs

Drawing on Photographs is a series of workshops we organized with primary and high school students. The workshops consisted of the short introduction presenting the history of colour in photography and the works of the artists that use drawing on photographs as their artistic expression.
Afterwards, we gave the students copies of the local XIX and XX century photographs and a lot of colours, and let them have fun! All of them took their works home.
