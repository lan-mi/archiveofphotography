Adopt a photograph

A lot of photographs capture our attention. Sometimes they are carefully preserved in public or private collections and sometimes they have a more personal value. Unfortunately, not all the photographs are lucky enough to have someone who cares for them.
We have decided to find the forgotten and the unwanted ones, and the photographs that are, for some reason, lost, and give them a new home.
Adopt a photograph is one of the dearest projects that we keep organizing year after year. The response from people is always positive and extremely rewarding. People often send us pictures of the photographs they adopted.
We are looking forward to finding a new home for these photographs.
