Adopt a photograph

A lot of photographs capture our attention. Sometimes they are carefully preserved in public or private collections and sometimes they have a more personal value. Unfortunately, not all the photographs are lucky enough to have someone who cares for them.
We have decided to find the forgotten ones, the unwanted and photographs that are for some reason lost and give them a new home.
Adopt a photograph is one of the dearest projects that we keep organizing year after year. The response from the people is always positive and very rewarding. People often send us pictures of adopted photographs in their new homes.
We are looking forward to finding a new home for these photographs.
