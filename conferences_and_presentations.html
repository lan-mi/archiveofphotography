Conferences

We believe that sharing experience and knowledge is of utmost importance when it comes to protecting and promoting photographic cultural heritage. Therefore, we highly value cooperation with other organizations and institutions. The Archive of Photography has, so far, organized and participated in various projects, conferences and presentations, held in Zrenjanin, Belgrade and Novi Sad.
