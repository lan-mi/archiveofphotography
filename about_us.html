ORGANIZATION

The Archive of Photography promotes photography as the cultural heritage by using: internet page, exhibitions, seminars, printed material, professional library etc. Participants in our activities include institutions and cultural organizations, private collectors and experts from the areas that deal with photography.

VISION

The vision of the Archive of Photography is a society in which photography is recognized as a significant cultural heritage â€“ including the adequate and continual care for it.

MISSION

We create conditions for keeping and taking care of photography as a cultural and intercultural heritage in Vojvodina region and wider, by digital preservation, collecting technical material, organizing researchers and having an interdisciplinary approach to photography.

STRATEGIC GOALS

1. Examining photographs from the perspective of history and theory of photography, culturology, humanities, etc.
2. Education
3. Promoting photography as a cultural and intercultural heritage
